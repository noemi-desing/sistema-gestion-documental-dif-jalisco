System Overview

This platform is designed to manage institutional correspondence and documentation for the Institutional Planning Directorate of DIF Jalisco. It ensures order, traceability, regulatory compliance, and institutional continuity throughout the entire administrative term.

System Functions

  • Daily registration of incoming and outgoing documents.
  • Management of memorandums, official letters, and circulars.
  • Automatic routing to Directorates and Departments.
  • Tracking of document processing by assistants and department heads.
  • Automatic folio generation and numbering control.
  • Automatic archival classification (user-independent).

Document Archive

The system manages both Active Files and Concentration Files in compliance with institutional archival regulations. Active documents are handled on a quarterly basis, while closed documents are preserved throughout the entire administrative term in read-only mode.

CADIDO Integration

CADIDO archival rules are integrated as a hidden system layer. Series, subseries, retention periods, documentary value, and final disposition are applied automatically. Users cannot view or modify CADIDO codes.

User Permissions

  • Department Heads and Assistants: Edit and respond to documents.
  • General Users: Read-only access.
  • General Directorate: Full consultation access.
